📊 Dataflow – Smart Vehicle Management System

Data enters the system through multiple entry points such as vehicle registration, service booking, and inventory updates.

Owner Data Entry
Data is first inserted into the OWNER table when a customer registers.
Each owner can register multiple vehicles in the VEHICLE table.
Vehicle Registration Flow
VEHICLE table depends on OWNER via OwnerID.
Every vehicle is uniquely identified and linked to its owner.
Appointment Flow
Customers book services in APPOINTMENT.
Each appointment is linked to VEHICLE, MECHANIC, and SERVICE_TYPE.
Service Execution Flow
When service is performed, a record is created in SERVICE.
SERVICE depends on VEHICLE, MECHANIC, and optionally APPOINTMENT.
Parts Usage Flow
During service, parts are recorded in SERVICE_PARTS.
This connects SERVICE and INVENTORY (many-to-many relationship).
Inventory Flow
INVENTORY stock is automatically reduced when parts are used.
Billing Flow
After service completion, an INVOICE is generated from SERVICE data.
Payments are recorded in PAYMENT linked to INVOICE.
🔚 Output of System:
Service history reports
Invoice summaries
Payment tracking
Vehicle maintenance records
