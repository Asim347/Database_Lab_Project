📊 Dataflow — Smart Vehicle Management System

Data enters the system through vehicle owners, vehicle registration, service appointments, inventory management, and payment processing. The system ensures structured movement of data across all related tables.

OWNER → VEHICLE: Owner details are stored first, and each owner can register multiple vehicles using OwnerID as a foreign key.
VEHICLE → APPOINTMENT: Registered vehicles are used to book service appointments, which include service type, date, and assigned mechanic.
APPOINTMENT → SERVICE: Once the appointment is completed, a service record is created to log actual work performed on the vehicle.
SERVICE → SERVICE_PARTS → INVENTORY: During service, required spare parts are recorded. This links SERVICE and INVENTORY tables and automatically updates stock levels.
SERVICE → INVOICE: After service completion, an invoice is generated containing cost details such as subtotal, tax, and total amount.
INVOICE → PAYMENT: Payments are recorded against invoices using different payment methods like cash, card, or bank transfer.
🎯 Output of the System:

The system produces service history, billing invoices, payment records, and inventory status reports.
